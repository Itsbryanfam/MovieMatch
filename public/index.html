          How to Play

          Chain movies together using shared cast members.

          1
          Name a Movie

          Type any movie or TV show into the search bar on your turn.

          2
          Connect the Cast

          Your pick must share at least one actor with the previous movie in the chain.

          3
          Beat the Clock

          You have up to 60 seconds per turn in Classic — less in Speed mode. The timer shrinks every two successful plays in Classic and Hardcore — don't freeze up!

          4
          Last One Standing

          If you can't name a valid connection in time, you're eliminated. The last player alive wins the round!

          🎮 Game Modes

          ⚔️ Classic — Last player standing wins. Timer shrinks every two plays.
          🤝 Team — 2v2 shared-turn action. If one player fails, the whole team goes down!
          🎯 Solo — Endless survival. Rack up the highest movie chain length possible.
          ⚡ Speed — Pure chaos. Exactly 15 seconds per turn for the entire game.

          ⚙️ Modifiers

          🔥 Hardcore Mode — once an actor has connected anywhere in the chain, they're locked out for the rest of the game.
          📺 TV Shows — expands the pool to include television series alongside films.
